Help on Simple Attendance System Developed by Mitch Halloran
for use at and inspired by Fresno City College

The Simple Attendance system is developed to use Google Forms to have students use their devices (smartphones, tablets, laptops, etc) to connect to an URL that opens a Google Form and to record their presence in a class (lecture, laboratory, etc) session, and for the instructor to take that data and check it against a roster, with the output to the instructor of which students are absent and present in the session.

A web app (written in TypeScript) was coded to take the input files needed and to present the results both on web page and for download as a CSV file.
